About Murtle

Tono, a combination of 'tortuga' and 'mono', is dedicated to bringing people from different cultures together. Our platform facilitates casual conversations that promote language learning and cultural exchange. Whether you're looking to learn a new language or simply connect with someone from a different part of the world, Murtle is here to make those connections easy and enjoyable.
